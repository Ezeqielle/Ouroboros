**Advertiser Ranks and Cuts:**
@Rare Advertiser: 10% 
@Epic Advertiser: 12% 
@Legendary Advertiser: 15%

If you wish to progress to the next rank of Advertiser, you will need to meet the following thresholds:
To reach @Epic Advertiser you must make 4M gold in one pay cycle.
To reach @Legendary Advertiser you must make 10M gold in one pay cycle.


Once you reach @Epic Advertiser you can keep it, unless you make no sales over two cycles.
@Legendary Advertiser will have to make at least 8M gold per cycle to keep the rank.
